First step towards your prediction

The model needs a few key characteristics of the house to make a good prediction of the sale price. With each characteristic, an explanation will be given to help you better describe the property.

1. Overall quality

This section measures the quality of the materials used in the house, and how polished the finished product is. Think about the glass, tiles and reinforcement. How well are they integrated and how long could the durability of these items be. Choose a number between 1 and 10, where the lower numbers (below 4) mean that there is need for a lot of renovation projects, and the higher number (above 7) mean that the property is of a high-class standard.
