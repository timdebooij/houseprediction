Are you in the market for a house?

In the Netherlands it has become nearly impossible to buy a house at a reasonable price. The market is overflowing with landlords that want to rent houses for a lot of money. Mostly, starting families and first-time buyers are victimized by this, but even the average person that wants to enter the market does not realize what they are up against. Not knowing what to bid is dangerous, since it can lead to long waiting periods before getting your dreamhouse, or pay too much in an attempt to overcompensate

The goal of this website is to protect you from these dangers and give an insight in the current prices of houses. We use an Artificial Intelligence model to predict the sale prices with just a few of the characteristics of the house. At the moment only a specific city is loaded in the model: Ames in Iowa, USA. In the ideal situation more data will be gathered to get multiple cities, or even complete countries in the model. After analysing the housing market in Ames, we will start gathering data for the big cities in the Netherlands.
